# User Guide: Scenario 2 - The Knowledge Guardian Cycle

This scenario demonstrates how the **Knowledge Guardian** feature empowers agents to identify outdated information, suggest corrections, and get rewarded for their contributionsâ€”all in real-time.

---

## Phase 1: Start the Simulation

1.  Navigate to the **Home Dashboard**.
2.  Locate the card for **"Scenario 2: The Knowledge Guardian Cycle"**.
3.  Click the **"Start Scenario 2"** button.

![Start Scenario 2](/Users/peti/.gemini/antigravity/brain/1f8e92a4-059a-4dd9-bfbb-f0bc10a91d6c/scenario2_start.png)

---

## Phase 2: Agent Interaction (Identify & Suggest)

You are now acting as **Alex**, a top-tier agent during a simulation training exercise.

1.  **Review the Document**: On the right side, you will see the "VIP Renewal Tiers - FINAL.pdf".
2.  **Find the Discrepancy**: Locate the section mentioning **"3% cashback"**. This is outdated; the new policy is 5%.
3.  **Select the Text**: Click and drag (or just click) on the text **"3% cashback"** to highlight it.
4.  **Suggest Update**: A **"Suggest Update"** button will appear floating near your selection. Click it.

5.  **Submit Feedback**: A modal will appear.
    *   **Original Text**: "3% cashback..."
    *   **Your Note**: A pre-filled correction note is provided for the demo.
    *   Click **"Submit for Review"**.

![Suggest Update Modal](/Users/peti/.gemini/antigravity/brain/1f8e92a4-059a-4dd9-bfbb-f0bc10a91d6c/scenario2_modal.png)

> **Result**: You will see a notification awarding you **+150 XP** for being proactive!

---

## Phase 3: Lead Approval (The Feedback Loop)

Now, switch roles to see how a Team Lead or Knowledge Manager handles this input.

1.  **Switch View**: In the left sidebar, click on **"Lead"**.
2.  **Review Alerts**: Look at the **"Knowledge Gap Alerts"** card. You will see the suggestion from Alex (Agent).
3.  **Approve**: Click the **"Approve & Sync"** button to validate the finding.

![Lead Approval](/Users/peti/.gemini/antigravity/brain/1f8e92a4-059a-4dd9-bfbb-f0bc10a91d6c/scenario2_lead.png)

> **Result**: The system instantly updates the knowledge base status and notifies the agent.

---

## Phase 4: Validating Rewards

Finally, confirm that the gamification loop is closed.

1.  **Return to Agent View**: Click on **"Agent"** in the sidebar.
2.  **Check Leaderboard**: Look at the **"December Leaderboard"**.
3.  **Verify Points**: Alex should now be at the **#1 spot** with a significant point boost (Bonus for approved contribution).

![Leaderboard Updated](/Users/peti/.gemini/antigravity/brain/1f8e92a4-059a-4dd9-bfbb-f0bc10a91d6c/scenario2_final.png)

---

**Summary**: You have successfully completed the Knowledge Guardian Cycle!
